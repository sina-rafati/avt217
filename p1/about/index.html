ABOUT

Established in 2001, located in Beverly Hills, Los Angeles, California. The Velvet Velvet Gallery presents limited edition works made through exclusive collaboration with prominent contemporary artists. We work with notable and iconic artists to make great art radically more accessible for our generation. The Velvet Velvet Gallery presents up to for extraordinary and highly publicized exhibitions annually that require acquisition of outstanding art and design. Also, The Velvet Velvet gallery provides monthly exhibit collaborating with local designers.
